Norfolk's most trusted surveyor

Norfolk Surveying and Design was established in February 2021 and at time of writing has worked on 363 projects ranging from small residential extensions to large commercial developments. Norfolk Surveying and Design is a surveying and Civil Engineering consultancy. Our core services include land surveying, civil engineering and design and site setting out. We have a diverse client base which includes architects, developers, builders, ground works companies and private residents.

Disciplines

Here at NSD we offer a number of different disciplines:

Land surveying

  • Topographical surveys
  • Site area calculations
  • Volume analysis
  • Flood risk surveys (to aid flood risk assesments)
  • Building floor plans
  • Building evaluations
  • As built asset plans
  • Structure movement monitoring
  • Conveyance plans
  • Boundary surveys (to aid Solicitors)

Civil engineering design

  • Highways design
  • Surface water drainage design
  • Foul water design
  • Soakaway sizing
  • Perculation testing
  • Site investigation (trial hole logs)
  • Foundation appraisals
  • Contamination reports
  • Suitability for conversion reports

  • Residential extensions

Site setting out

  • Setting out of buildings

  • Setting out of bolt boxes
  • Setting out of roads

  • Setting out of earth works
